### Miso-Tamarind Tacos
**Ingredients:**
- Corn tortillas
- Miso paste (white or yellow)
- Tamarind paste
- Shredded cabbage (for crunch)
- Fresh cilantro (for garnish)
- Pickled daikon radish (for a tangy twist)
- Avocado (for creaminess)
- Lime juice
- Sesame seeds (for a nutty finish)

### Preparation Steps:
1. **Marinade the Protein**: Choose your favorite protein (tofu, chicken, or shrimp). Mix equal parts miso and tamarind paste to create a flavorful marinade. Let your protein soak in this mixture for at least 30 minutes.

2. **Pickled Daikon**: Slice daikon radish thinly and soak in a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and a pinch of salt for about an hour. This adds a refreshing crunch!

3. **Avocado Cream**: Blend ripe avocados with lime juice, a bit of salt, and a drizzle of sesame oil until smooth. This will be your creamy topping.

4. **Cook the Protein**: Grill or sautÃ© your marinated protein until cooked through and nicely caramelized.

5. **Assemble the Tacos**: Warm the corn tortillas and layer them with shredded cabbage, your protein, pickled daikon, and a generous dollop of avocado cream. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and fresh cilantro.
